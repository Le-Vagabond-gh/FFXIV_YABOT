# Pomander List

While inside a deep dungeon (Palace of the Dead, Heaven-on-High, or Eureka Orthos), shows a small overlay listing the pomanders and magicite/demiclones you currently hold. Each row shows the in-game icon, the canonical name ("Pomander of X", "X Magicite", or "X Demiclone"), a two-word effect summary, and the quantity (`x2`, `x3`...). Clicking a row uses that pomander/stone immediately - the same as right-clicking it in the in-game pomander menu. Pomanders already active on the current floor are highlighted in green. Hold Shift to drag the window to reposition it.

The dungeon contents and current counts come from `EventFramework.GetInstanceContentDeepDungeon()` (the live `InstanceContentDeepDungeon` struct - `Items[16]` for pomanders, `Magicite[3]` for magic stones/demiclones). The slot-to-name mapping is taken from the `DeepDungeon` Excel sheet's `PomanderSlot`/`MagiciteSlot` arrays - magic stones for PotD/HoH (DeepDungeonType 1) and demiclones for Eureka Orthos (DeepDungeonType 2). Clicks call the engine's `UsePomander(slot)` / `UseStone(slot)` directly.
