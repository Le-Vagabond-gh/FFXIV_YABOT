# Auto Display Name Settings

Automatically sets the "Display name plates of other PCs" option based on your current zone. Each context gets its own preference (Always, During Battle, When Targeted, or Never):

- **In Cities** - city areas like Limsa, Ul'dah, etc.
- **Outside of Cities** - regular open-world zones.
- **In Duty** - standard duty content entered via the Duty Finder (dungeons, trials, raids, etc.).
- **In Deep Dungeons** - Palace of the Dead, Heaven-on-High, and Eureka Orthos.
- **In Large-Scale Duty** - high-player-count content: alliance raids (24 players), Eureka, Bozja (Bozjan Southern Front / Zadnor / Delubrum Reginae), and Occult Crescent (up to 72 players).

The large-scale and deep dungeon checks take precedence over the regular duty check, so these zones always use their own setting even though they are technically duties.

In deep dungeons the players matched with you are party members, so the deep dungeon setting drives the party name plate option rather than the "other PCs" one. Your original party setting is restored when you leave.
