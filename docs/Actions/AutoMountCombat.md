# Auto-Mount After Combat

Mounts your character automatically after combat ends. Supports mount roulette or a specific selected mount, optional auto-jump on flying maps, and exclusions for housing zones, Occult Crescent, and active FATEs. In Occult Crescent it can use Return + auto-confirm instead of mounting. Can also auto-mount after an aetheryte teleport lands you in Occult Crescent (independent of the post-combat exclusion). Blocks teleports while a mount cast is in progress to prevent stuck-teleport state.
